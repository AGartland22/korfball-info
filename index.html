What is Korfball?

A Brief History of Korf

Korfball is a ball sport invented in the Netherlands in 1902 by a Dutch schoolteacher, Nico Broekhuysen. Today it's played in over 70 countries.

What Are the Rules?

Two teams of 8 players (4 male, 4 female) work together to score by shooting through a 3.5m hoop. No dribbling is allowed, you can't shoot when another player of the same gender is nearby and players of opposite genders can't mark each other. Click on the YouTube link in the footer to see what a game of Korfball looks like.

Why Play Korfball?

Korfball is fantastic for fitness and social reasons. It's unique in that all players are equal and teams are only separated based on ability, not age or gender. There's something for everyone.
